Publications

As a contributing writer

“Five Out of Five Hearts”, winner of the Switchgrass Review first place fiction award. Published in Vol 2 Nov 5, 2015 by the Coastal Bend Wellness Foundation. www.cbwellness.org
"Five out of Five Hearts" follows the post-college years of Linda and Paul, two friends in the LGBTQIA+ community who share a dualism of character, gender identity, and relationship trajectory. Through a series of five vingettes, woven together with the techno-texture of social media and device mediation, we see the fracturing of a friendship that cannot be sustained on the purity of sameness without cross-examination. Readers have called the story a 'romantic' tragedy while I view it as a platonic one, which makes the characters non-the-less tragic or star-crossed. Paul is Linda's alternate life path.

“Penultimate Myth”, honorable mention at the prestigious Scissortail Poetry Festival. Published in the Island Waves Student Newspaper, Vol 26, Issue 11, Pg 2, 10 April 2014. Island Waves
"Penultimate Myth" signified a return to poetry for me, not in the sense that I hadn't written poetry just prior to that, but rather that I reinvented poetry in my life. It was now something I was compelled to share. This poem was written as a letter to someone I had a crush on but it was decidedly not a love poem. Rather, it was because I had such admiration and trust for that person, that communication with them, through this poem, was the pipeline of catharsis that I needed, not only to tap the tumult of my situation, but to compose it into an artwork. This was how I found stability.
